Serrala is the global leader in finance process automation. For over 40 years, we have been advancing the office of the CFO with our award-winning suite of finance automation applications.

Join us as we lead the future of finance automation!


Shape the future of AI in enterprise finance.



Serrala is building an Agentic AI Execution Layer to deliver production-grade AI agents embedded into finance workflows across Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, and Payments & Cash.


The AI Engineer will build production-grade agents (not demos) - from single-purpose expert agents to orchestrated multi-agent workflows, using Serrala’s standardized AI platform patterns (RAG, tool calling, memory, evaluation, observability, and governance).


The role requires a strong engineering mindset: reliable behavior, finance-grade failure handling, human-in-the-loop controls, and measurable performance across cost, latency, and quality.


This role operates within Serrala where the central AI team owns reusable AI services (gateway, RAG, orchestration, evaluation, guardrails, observability/FinOps) and domain teams productize outcomes.


Your Day to Day:



Build production-grade AI agents (single → multi-agent workflows) with reliable reasoning, tool usage, state handling, and safe fallbacks.
Design auditable, finance-grade workflows with guardrails, approvals, explainability, and full traceability.
Implement RAG, tool calling, and memory patterns grounded in enterprise data
Instrument agents with telemetry & evaluation to optimize quality, cost, latency, and reliability.
Integrate agents into SAP-embedded and SaaS products, following enterprise security and rollout standards.
Ship with excellence: CI/CD, logging, diagnostics, and quality gates.


What makes you excel in this position:



4+ years of software engineering experience, incl. 1–2+ years with LLM/agentic systems.
Strong hands-on skills in prompting, RAG, orchestration, tool calling, and reliability patterns.
Experience with deterministic + probabilistic systems and human-in-the-loop flows.
Solid engineering fundamentals and comfort in enterprise environments (auditability, access control).
Finance / ERP experience (AR, AP, Payments) is strongly preferred.
LLM evaluation frameworks and agent orchestration patterns are a strong plus.
You build production systems, not demos.
You think in controls, failure modes, and trust.
You balance speed, correctness, and cost.
